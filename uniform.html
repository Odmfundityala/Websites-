Uniform & Sports

School Uniform Requirements and Sports Activities

School Uniform

Our uniform represents our school's identity and promotes a sense of unity and pride

Girls Uniform

  • Sky blue dresses
  • Navy blazer (with white stripes or plain navy)
  • White socks
  • Black school shoes

Boys Uniform

  • White shirts
  • Navy jeans
  • Navy blazer (with white stripes or plain navy)
  • Black school shoes

Additional Items

  • Navy school jersey (winter)
  • School tie (optional)
  • Navy school beanie (winter)
  • School badge

Sports Activities

We offer various sporting activities to promote physical fitness and team spirit

Soccer

Our soccer teams participate in local and regional competitions, promoting teamwork and athletic excellence.

  • Boys and Girls Teams
  • Regular Training Sessions
  • Inter-school Competitions

Netball

Our netball program focuses on developing skills, strategy, and team coordination.

  • Girls Teams
  • Professional Coaching
  • Regional Tournaments

Baseball

Our baseball program teaches discipline, precision, and strategic thinking.

  • Mixed Teams
  • Regular Practice Sessions
  • Competitive Matches

Required Sports Kit

  • School sports t-shirt (sky blue)
  • Navy sports shorts/skirts
  • Appropriate sports shoes
  • Team jerseys (provided for matches)

Note: Specific sports equipment will be communicated by respective coaches
