School Fees

Siya - Quality education accessible to all, breaking barriers from Grade 10 to Grade 12

No-Fee School

At Siya, we believe that financial barriers should never limit a student's potential! We are proudly a no-fee institution, ensuring that every deserving student can access world-class education and pursue their dreams without compromise.

No Tuition Fees

As a Quintile 3 public school, we do not charge any tuition fees to our students.

Government Funded

We receive budget allocation from the Department of Basic Education to support our educational programs.

Community Partnership

Parents are encouraged to support the school as strategic partners in education excellence.

School Funding

Government Budget Allocation

Siya receives budget allocation from the Department of Basic Education, enabling us to maintain our unwavering commitment to excellence. This partnership fuels our mission to transform lives through education and build tomorrow's leaders.

School Fundraising Programs

To bridge the funding gap and enhance educational opportunities, the school operates various fundraising programs determined by the School Governing Body. These programs may include:

  • Community fundraising events
  • Educational enhancement programs
  • Infrastructure improvement initiatives
  • Training and development programs
  • Cash contributions per student from the community

Parent Partnership & Contributions

Strategic Partnership

Join us as champions of education! Parents are invited to become strategic partners in this incredible journey of transformation. Your annual cash contributions per student create opportunities that turn dreams into reality and help fund:

  • Enhanced learning materials and resources
  • Educational technology and equipment
  • Teacher training and development programs
  • School maintenance and improvements
  • Extracurricular activities and sports programs

Additional Expenses

While there are no tuition fees, families may need to provide:

  • School uniform
  • Sports equipment (if participating in sports)
  • Additional stationery items
  • Transport to and from school
  • Optional school trips and excursions
