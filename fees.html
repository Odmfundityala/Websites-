School Fees & Funding

Siya - Quality education accessible to all, breaking barriers from Grade 10 to Grade 12

No-Fee School Policy

Siyabulela Senior Secondary School is proud to be a no-fee institution, making quality education accessible to all eligible students in our community. We are committed to providing excellent education without financial barriers.

No Tuition Fees

As a Quintile 3 public school, we do not charge any tuition fees to our students, ensuring education remains accessible to all.

Government Funded

We receive up to R1,602 per learner from the Department of Education for operational expenses and educational resources.

Training Programmes

Special funding for skills development and training programmes, determined by our School Governing Body.

School Funding Sources

Government Allocation

As a Quintile 3 institution, we receive substantial government funding that covers:

  • Learning materials and textbooks for all subjects
  • School maintenance and utility costs
  • Educational equipment and technology
  • Administrative and operational expenses
  • Basic school supplies and stationery
  • Infrastructure development and improvements

Training Programme Funding

Our School Governing Body allocates special funding for:

  • Skills development workshops for learners
  • Professional development for educators
  • Career guidance and counseling programmes
  • Computer literacy and digital skills training
  • Leadership development initiatives
  • Community engagement projects

Note: Training programme funding is determined by the School Governing Body from time to time and may include cash contributions from various sources.

Optional Expenses

Personal Items

While tuition is free, families may need to provide:

  • School uniform (sky blue dresses for girls, white shirts and navy jeans for boys)
  • Navy blazers with school insignia
  • Black school shoes and white socks
  • Personal stationery items
  • Sports equipment for extracurricular activities
  • Transportation to and from school

Support Available

For families requiring assistance:

  • Contact school administration for financial guidance
  • Explore available government support programs
  • Discuss flexible payment arrangements
  • Access to uniform assistance programmes
  • Transportation support initiatives
  • Scholarship opportunities for exceptional students

School Governing Body

Financial Oversight

Our School Governing Body ensures transparent and effective management of all school funds, including special allocations for training and development programmes.

Fund Allocation

The SGB regularly reviews and determines funding priorities, ensuring resources are directed towards programmes that benefit our learners and community.

Community Contributions

When appropriate, the SGB may approve cash contributions from community partners to enhance our training and development offerings.
