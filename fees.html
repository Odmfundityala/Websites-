School Fees

Information about our no-fee policy

No-Fee School

Siyabulela Senior Secondary School is proud to be a no-fee institution, making quality education accessible to all eligible students in our community.

No Tuition Fees

As a Quintile 3 public school, we do not charge any tuition fees to our students.

Government Funded

We receive up to R1,602 per learner from the Department of Education for operational expenses.

Educational Resources

Government funding covers essential educational resources and operational costs.

School Funding

Quintile 3 Classification

Our school is classified as Quintile 3, which means we serve a community with moderate socio-economic status. This classification determines the level of government funding we receive to ensure quality education for all students.

Government Support

The Department of Education provides funding to cover:

  • Learning materials and textbooks
  • School maintenance and utilities
  • Educational equipment
  • Administrative costs
  • Basic school supplies

Additional Information

Optional Costs

While there are no tuition fees, parents may need to provide:

  • School uniform
  • Sports equipment (if participating in sports)
  • Additional stationery items
  • Transport to and from school

Financial Assistance

For families needing assistance with additional costs:

  • Contact the school administration
  • Explore available support programs
  • Discuss payment arrangements if needed
