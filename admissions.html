Admission Requirements - Your Path to Success

Grade 10 Entry

  • Successful completion of Grade 9
  • School report with satisfactory progress
  • Birth certificate or ID document
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of residence

Grade 11 & 12 Transfers

  • Previous school academic records
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Birth certificate or ID document
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of residence
