We Accept Students

Join our school family and grow with us through your educational journey

Grade 8 to Grade 11

Admission Requirements

  • Transfer Letter from previous school (primary requirement)
  • Birth Certificate or ID document (required if no transfer letter is available)
  • Previous School Report showing academic progress
  • Proof of Residence (utility bill or affidavit)
  • Passport-sized Photographs (2 recent photos)

Important: We do not admit Grade 12 students. At Siya, we believe in nurturing our learners from earlier grades, allowing us to build strong foundations and guide them through to their Matric year.
