Admissions

Siya - Your gateway to excellence and a brighter future through Grades 10 to 12

Admission Process

The admission to Siyabulela Senior Secondary School follows the structured process outlined by the South African Schools Act of 1996. We are committed to providing quality education to all eligible students.

Registration Period

The applications and egistrations for the upcoming academic year takes place between October and February of the preceding academic year

Age Requirements

Children between the ages of 7 and 15 are mandated to attend school as per South African law.

Required Documents

Essential documents include birth certificates and immunization cards. Additional documentation required for non-South African citizens.

Application Process

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form or walk in along with all required documents:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Reports
  • Immunization Card
  • Parents/Guardian ID
  • Proof of Residence
2

Document Verification

School administration will verify all submitted documents and check for completeness.

3

Application Review

Applications are reviewed based on admission criteria and available space.

4

Notification

Parents/guardians will be notified of the admission decision.
