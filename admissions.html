Admissions

Join our family of future leaders - we accept students from Grade 8 to Grade 11

Requirements

Primary Document

  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • If unavailable: Birth certificate or ID

Supporting Documents

  • Previous school report
  • Proof of residence
  • 2 passport-sized photos

Note: We do not admit Grade 12 students. We nurture learners from earlier grades, building strong foundations through to Matric.
