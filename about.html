Our School

Siyabulela Senior Secondary School is a public secondary school situated at Ngcisininde A/A Ndiki Location, Nqamakwe, 4990. We are proud to serve the Ngcisininde A/A, Nqamakwe, Nqamakhwe rural suburb of Eastern Cape Province, South Africa.

Our Community

We are a vibrant educational community dedicated to nurturing academic excellence and personal growth in our students.

Academic Excellence

With a student-teacher ratio of 21:1, we ensure personalized attention and support for each learner.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to society.
