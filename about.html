About Our School

Siya - Nurturing tomorrow's leaders through transformative education, Grades 10 to 12

Our School

Siya stands as a beacon of hope and excellence in the heart of Ngcisininde A/A Ndiki Location, Nqamakwe. We are more than a school - we are a community of dreamers, achievers, and future changemakers, proudly transforming lives in the Eastern Cape Province, South Africa.

Our Inspiring Community

We are a family of believers, achievers, and dream-builders! Together, we create an environment where every student discovers their greatness and reaches for the stars.

Excellence in Every Step

With our 21:1 student-teacher ratio, every learner receives the personal guidance needed to unlock their unique brilliance and soar beyond expectations.

Our Powerful Mission

To ignite minds, inspire hearts, and empower every student to not just achieve their dreams, but to become the positive change our world desperately needs!
